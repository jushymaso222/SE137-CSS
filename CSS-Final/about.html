About Me

Hi, my name is Josh and I'm a software engineer currently pursuing a bachelors degree at New England Tech. I've been coding for 7 years now with languages ranging from LUA, Python, C#, Javascript, HTML & CSS, and more. I'm constantly trying to learn new languages whenever I can. I'm starting to learn React for future projects of mine.

I started off when I was young playing Minecraft with a mod called ComputerCraft. COmputerCraft is a Minecraft modo that uses LUA to interact with the world around you through coding. It started with simple projects like making a security door, or a basic messaging system between computers, and escalated out into what I'm doing today. Minecraft was a great way to get into coding due to the ability to see your coding affects on the 3D world around you. It was very low risk and high reward which is what got me hooked.

I began college at URI as a mechanical engineer. I wanted to go to a college in Florida proceeding URI for a degree in aerospace engineering but didn't like the teaching at URI. The campus was beautiful and the people were nice but I just wasn't learning how I wanted to learn. I had a couple friends at New England Tech; one in for drone technology, and one for video game design; so I decided to give it a shot and I have been loving my time here. In March of next year I'll be starting the bachelors courses and I'll be on my way to my degree!

In my free times, I have a couple hobbies that keep me busy:

  • Video Games (probably too many video games but its a good time killer)
  • Coding self-projects
  • Golfing, Karting, and Climbing
  • Spending time with my friends, family and girlfriend
  • Sleeping (I do a lot of this)
